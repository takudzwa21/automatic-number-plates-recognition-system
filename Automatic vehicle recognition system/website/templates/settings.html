Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I log-in to the system?
A: To log in the system, you must have an account created in your details. Navigate to the log in button and fill you user name and password to access the system.
Q: How do I create an account?
A: To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill out the registration form.
Q: How to start the System?
A: Our system is designed with easy-to-use interface, where on the home page you just need to press the "start system" button. Then the system starts automatically. Now press the "Start Camera" button to start using the camera. Note that you need to have a camera connected to you device.
Q: How do I add new vehicle?
A: To add new vehicle, click on "Add new vehicle" button on the screen and fill the two forms that will appear on the screen. The one is for the vehicle details and the other is for the vehicle owner details.

Common Issues and Solutions

  • Issue: Not recognizing vehicles properly
  • Solution: Ensure proper lighting conditions and camera angle. Make sure the vehicle is within the camera's view.
  • Issue: Slow processing time
  • Solution: Check system resources and optimize if necessary. Ensure a stable internet connection for cloud-based processing.
  • Issue: Error messages during recognition
  • Solution: Refer to the error message for specific instructions. If unclear, contact support for assistance.
