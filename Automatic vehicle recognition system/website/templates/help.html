About Us

Welcome to our Vehicle Recognition System! We are dedicated to providing cutting-edge technology to automate the process of vehicle recognition. We are currently working as a group of students and we made this for the sake of our learning as well as to demostrate our potential and hence this project enables us to gain a vast amount of practical experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify and enhance the process of vehicle recognition for various applications, including security, traffic management, and more. We aim to provide accurate and efficient solutions to meet the evolving needs of our customers. The main mission of this specific project is to automate the process of manual recognition of Vehicles here at Africa University gate.

Our Team

We are a team of passionate computer science students. Together, we strive to innovate and deliver high-quality products that make a difference.
